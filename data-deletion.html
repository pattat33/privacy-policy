Data Deletion Instructions

We respect your right to request deletion of your data. Here's how you can request data deletion:

Option 1: Through the App

  1. Log into your account
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Select "Delete My Data"
  4. Confirm your choice

Option 2: Contact Us Directly

Send an email to patbuysstuff@gmail.com with:

  • Subject: "Data Deletion Request"
  • Your username
  • The email associated with your account

We will process your request within 30 days.

What We Delete

  • Your profile information
  • Your Instagram connection data
  • Any content you've created
  • Usage history and preferences

Questions?

If you have any questions about data deletion, please contact us at patbuysstuff@gmail.com
